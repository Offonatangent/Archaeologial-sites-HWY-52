Nicholasville has grown since it’s founding in 1812, but it’s down town area looks much the same as it did 80 years ago. Located just south of Lexington, Kentucky, downtown Nicholasville boasts numerous restaurants, bars, and even a local coffee shop all within a block of the court house. The north side of town has developed to include eateries of most any type accessible by sidewalks along Main Street. Lake Mingo and Riney B Park have lovely walking trails with frisbee golf courses all within a mile of these dining areas. So visit scenic Nicholasville! Play-Eat-Walk, you are never more than a mile from Park or restaurant.
